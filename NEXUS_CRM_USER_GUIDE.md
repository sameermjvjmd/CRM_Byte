## 1. Introduction
Nexus CRM is a comprehensive relationship management platform designed to streamline your sales, marketing, and business operations. It unifies contact management, sales pipeline tracking, and customer communication into a single interface.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### Navigation
The **Sidebar** on the left is your main navigation hub. It is organized into logical groups:
*   **Core**: Dashboard, Contacts, Companies, Groups.
*   **Sales**: Opportunities (Pipeline), Schedule, History.
*   **Marketing**: Campaigns, Templates.
*   **Tools**: Import, Export, Reports.
*   **Settings**: Admin configurations.

### Global Search
Use the **Search Bar** (or `Ctrl+K`) at the top to instantly find Contacts, Companies, or Opportunities by name, email, or phone number.

## 3. Dashboard & Analytics
The **Dashboard** provides a real-time overview of your business health:
*   **Sales Performance**: Total revenue, deal count, and win rate.
*   **Pipeline Health**: Visual distribution of deals by stage.
*   **Recent Activity**: Latest interactions and updates.
*   **Tasks**: Upcoming to-dos and reminders.

## 4. Contacts Management
Contacts are the core entities in Nexus CRM.

### Creating Contacts
1.  Click the **+ (Plus)** button in the top toolbar or the "New Contact" button on the Contacts page.
2.  **General Info**: Enter First Name, Last Name, Email, and Phone.
3.  **Additional Details**: Custom attributes (see below) appear here.
4.  Click **Create Record**.

### Contact Custom Fields
You can extend contact profiles with custom data specific to your business (e.g., "Budget", "Customer Tier").
*   **Supported Types**: Text, Number, Currency, Percentage, Date, Checkbox, Select (Dropdown), Multi-Select, Email, URL.
*   **Viewing**: Custom fields appear in the "Extended Profile" section on the Contact Detail page sidebar and the "Custom Fields" tab.
*   **Editing**: Click "Edit" on a contact, scroll to the Custom Fields section, modify values, and save.

### Filters and Views
*   **Layouts**: Toggle between List View and Board View.
*   **Advanced Filter**: Use the filter icon to search by specific fields (e.g., "City equals New York" AND "Industry contains Tech").

## 5. Company & Group Management
*   **Companies**: Manage B2B relationships. Link multiple Contacts to a single Company.
*   **Groups**: Organize contacts into static or dynamic lists (e.g., "VIP Customers", "Newsletter Subscribers").
    *   **Dynamic Groups**: define rules (e.g., "Status = Active") to automatically populate the group.

---

## 6. Pipeline & Opportunities
Manage your sales process visually.
*   **Pipeline Board**: Drag and drop deals between stages (e.g., Initial -> Qualified -> Proposal -> Closed Won).
*   **Forecasting**: Predict future revenue based on deal probability and close dates.
*   **Deal Velocity**: Analyze how fast deals move through the pipeline to identify bottlenecks.
*   **Win/Loss Analysis**: Review closed deals to understand success factors.

---

## 7. Activities & Calendar
*   **Schedule**: View upcoming Calls, Meetings, and Tasks in a Calendar or List view.
*   **History**: An immutable log of all past interactions (emails sent, meetings held, notes taken).
*   **Tasks**: A prioritized to-do list with due dates and reminders.

---

## 8. Marketing & Communication
*   **Email Templates**: Create reusable email designs and snippets.
*   **Signatures**: Manage professional email signatures for users.
*   **Landing Page Builder**: Visually design web pages for campaigns using a drag-and-drop editor.
*   **Lead Assignment Rules**: Automatically route new leads to sales reps based on criteria (e.g., Region, Source).
*   **SMS**: Send and receive text messages (requires gateway configuration).

---

## 9. Products & Quotes
*   **Products**: Maintain a catalog of items or services with pricing.
*   **Quotes**: Generate professional PDF quotes for Opportunities.
    *   Add line items from the Product catalog.
    *   Apply discounts and taxes.
    *   Email quotes directly to clients via a secure public link.

---

## 10. Tools & Utilities
*   **Import Data**: Bulk upload Contacts/Companies from CSV or Excel files.
*   **Export Data**: Download your data for backup or analysis.
*   **Duplicate Scanner**: Identify and merge duplicate records to keep data clean.
*   **Reports**: Generate visual reports on Sales, Activities, and Database growth.
*   **Lookup**: Quick-reference tools for postal codes or other datasets.

---

## 11. Administration
*   **User Management**: Add users, assign roles (Admin/User), and manage access.
*   **Role Management**: Define granular permissions (Read/Write/Delete) for each module.
*   **Custom Fields Manager**:
    *   Navigate to **Admin > Custom Fields**.
    *   Create new fields for Contacts, Companies, or Opportunities.
    *   Define field type (e.g., Dropdown options) and validation rules (Required/Optional).
*   **Email Settings**: Configure SMTP servers and tracking options.
*   **Workflows**: Automate repetitive tasks (e.g., "Send update email when Stage changes to Closed Won").

---

## 12. Troubleshooting

### Updates & Caching
*   If you encounter errors immediately after a system update (e.g., "Validation Error" on creation), try **Restarting the Application Server**. This ensures the running application matches the latest code structure.

### Search Issues
*   If generic search returns no results, try **Advanced Search** filters for precise field targeting.
